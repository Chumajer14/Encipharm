## Brand & Style

The design system is engineered for high-stakes B2B environments within the animal health, nutrition, and biosecurity sectors. The brand personality is **Corporate, Technical, and Clinical**, prioritizing clarity and data integrity over decorative elements. It targets professionals in sales management and laboratory logistics who require a high-density information display that remains legible and organized.

The aesthetic follows a **Modern Corporate** style with a focus on:
- **Clinical Cleanliness:** Utilizing ample whitespace and a "Sanitary Green" palette to evoke biosecurity standards.
- **Data-First Hierarchy:** A structured layout that emphasizes quantitative metrics and pipeline status.
- **Professional Trust:** A sober, reliable interface that avoids trends like neomorphism or neon accents in favor of subtle elevation and crisp borders.

## Colors

The color strategy is divided into functional zones to aid navigation in a complex CRM environment. 

- **Primary Institutional Green (#008C5A):** Used for primary actions, success states, and brand reinforcement.
- **Deep Navy Navigation (#102A43):** Reserved for high-level application structure (Sidebar) to provide a stable anchor for the user.
- **Technical Teal (#007C89):** Used for secondary interactive elements and data visualization categories.
- **Background & Surfaces:** The system uses a layering of `Background Gray` (#F6F8FA) for the canvas and `Surface White` (#FFFFFF) for interactive cards to create immediate visual separation.
- **Status Semantic Palette:** Specific colors are mapped to sales stages (ProspecciÃ³n through Cierre) to allow for instant visual scanning of the pipeline.

## Typography

This design system utilizes **Inter** for all functional UI and body text due to its exceptional legibility and neutral character. **IBM Plex Sans** is introduced for labels and technical metadata to provide a subtle "engineered" feel, characteristic of laboratory and technical documentation.

Headlines should be set with tighter letter-spacing to maintain a professional, compact appearance. Body text defaults to `#1F2933` (Primary Text Gray), while secondary information and "muted" labels use `#6B7280`. Large display sizes are scaled down on mobile devices to prevent layout breaking in data-heavy CRM views.

## Layout & Spacing

The design system employs a **Fixed-Fluid Hybrid Grid**. 
- **Navigation:** A fixed left-hand sidebar (260px) provides persistent access to Sales, Logistics, and Animal Data.
- **Content Area:** A fluid 12-column grid with 16px gutters. In data-heavy views (Dashboards), cards should span 3, 4, or 6 columns.
- **Spacing Rhythm:** Based on a 4px scale. Standard padding for cards is 20px (5 units) to maintain the "generous whitespace" requested, preventing the CRM from feeling claustrophobic.
- **Mobile Adaptivity:** On mobile, the sidebar collapses into a bottom-sheet or hamburger menu, and grid columns stack into a single-column layout with 16px horizontal margins.

## Elevation & Depth

Visual hierarchy is achieved through **Tonal Layering and Soft Elevation**. 
- **Level 0 (Background):** `#F6F8FA` â€” The lowest layer.
- **Level 1 (Cards/Surfaces):** `#FFFFFF` with a 1px border of `#E5E7EB`. This is the primary surface for content.
- **Shadows:** Use highly diffused, low-opacity shadows for interactive cards. (Example: `0px 4px 12px rgba(16, 42, 67, 0.05)`).
- **Interactive States:** On hover, a card's shadow should slightly intensify, and the border may transition to `Technical Teal` to indicate clickability.
- **Sidebar:** While it uses the `Deep Navy` color, it remains flat to appear as part of the application's structural "shell" rather than a floating element.

## Shapes

The shape language is defined by **Soft Precision**. A standard border radius of **12px** (Level 2) is applied to all cards, modal containers, and large input groups to soften the technical nature of the data. 

Smaller elements like buttons and badges use a slightly reduced radius (8px) or remain at 12px for consistency, ensuring the UI feels approachable yet professional. Elements should never be fully sharp (0px) nor fully pill-shaped (except for specific status tags), maintaining a disciplined, geometric appearance.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid `#008C5A` with white text. 12px corner radius.
- **Secondary:** Outlined `#007C89` with 1px stroke.
- **Tertiary:** Ghost style for low-priority actions (e.g., "Cancel").

### Cards
- **Base:** White background, 1px `#E5E7EB` border, 12px radius.
- **Header:** Often includes a subtle 1px bottom border to separate the title from content.

### Badges (Sales Pipeline)
Badges use a "Soft Background" style: a light tint of the color with dark text for high legibility.
- **ProspecciÃ³n:** Blue (#334E68 background tint).
- **CalificaciÃ³n:** Teal (#007C89).
- **Propuesta:** Yellow (#F5B942).
- **NegociaciÃ³n:** Orange (#F97316).
- **Cierre:** Green (#008C5A).

### Input Fields
- White background, 1px `#E5E7EB` border.
- On focus: border changes to `#008C5A` with a 2px "Soft Sanitary Green" glow.
- Labels are positioned above the field in `IBM Plex Sans` (Label-md).

### Charts & Icons
- **Charts:** Use a palette of Institutional Green, Technical Teal, and Deep Navy. Avoid "hot" colors unless indicating a "Critical Risk."
- **Icons:** 24px linear icons with a 1.5pt stroke weight. Subject matter includes animal silhouettes, laboratory flasks, and logistics trucks, rendered in `Deep Navy` or `Secondary Text Gray`.
